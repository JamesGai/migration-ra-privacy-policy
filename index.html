ElderLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

This Privacy Policy explains how ElderLink collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the ElderLink mobile application.

ElderLink is an elderly-care companion app that supports communication, reminders, AI chat, location sharing, daily summaries, and caregiver/elderly connection workflows.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use ElderLink:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, Firebase user ID, profile image, and selected role.
  • Profile information that you provide during onboarding or while using the app.
  • Caregiver and elderly connection information, including connection requests and linked user relationships.
  • Location information, if you grant location permission, to support map and location-sharing features.
  • Chat messages, AI conversation history, reminders, game activity, and daily summary content.
  • Voice or audio data when you use speech, transcription, voice, or avatar-related features.
  • Device and app information needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Authenticate users with Firebase and Google sign-in.
  • Provide caregiver and elderly user experiences.
  • Show location and map features when permission is granted.
  • Create, update, and display reminders.
  • Provide AI chat, memory, game, voice, transcription, avatar, and daily summary features.
  • Maintain app security, troubleshoot errors, and improve app reliability.
  • Comply with legal, safety, and platform requirements.

Location Data

ElderLink may collect precise or approximate location data when you grant location permission. Location data is used to support app features such as caregiver map views and location history. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, but some app features may stop working.

Voice and Audio Data

If you use voice, speech-to-text, text-to-speech, or avatar features, ElderLink may process voice or audio data to provide those features. Audio-related data may be sent to backend services or third-party service providers that perform transcription, speech, avatar, or AI processing.

Third-Party Services

ElderLink may use third-party services to operate app features, including:

  • Google Firebase and Google sign-in for authentication and app services.
  • Backend hosting and database services for storing app data.
  • AI and language model providers for chat, summaries, memory, and game features.
  • Speech, transcription, voice, and avatar service providers.
  • Map, geocoding, weather, or location-related service providers.

These providers may process information as needed to provide their services to ElderLink.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers that help operate ElderLink.
  • Between connected caregiver and elderly accounts, where required by app functionality.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or platform policy.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or others.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. Data transmitted between the app and backend services is intended to be protected using HTTPS or similar secure transport methods. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide ElderLink, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and support app functionality. You may request deletion of your account or associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

ElderLink is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Review and update profile information in the app where supported.
  • Disable location, microphone, camera, or notification permissions through your device settings.
  • Stop using Google sign-in by signing out of the app.
  • Request account or data deletion by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
